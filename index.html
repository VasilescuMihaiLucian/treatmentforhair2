ABOUT THE FORGET WHITE PRODUCT
Forget White Fitokeratine is a 100% natural formula, suitable for all hair types.
✓Repigments hairs - scalp, sideburns, eyebrows
✓Moisturizes the scalp and eliminates dandruff
✓Strengthens the root of the hair
✓Slows down hair loss


The blend of Forget White oils, a balanced combination of antioxidants and unsaturated fatty acids, acts to insulate and protect the root of the hair so that the oxidation process of MELANIN (the pigment that gives color to the hair) is prevented. By stopping the oxidation process, the melanin can act again and the hair regains its natural color.

What is new in this product is the introduction of one of the most powerful antioxidants known in the scientific world today, RESVERATROL, which protects and catalyzes the production of biochemical reactions in the skin complex.

The Forget White formula, used daily, intensifies blood circulation in the scalp area and contributes to the process of deep hydration of the scalp and hair follicles, improving the quality of the skin and hair.

INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE
The product is applied on clean and perfectly dry scalp. It can be applied directly to the scalp or a few drops can be applied to the palms and then gently massaged with the tips of the fingers on the entire surface of the scalp, allocating at least two minutes to each area. For optimal results, it is necessary to use 100ml of solution. The application is made a maximum of twice a day, daily, allocating at least 8 hours between applications, and preferably 12 hours before a new head wash. Washing before each application is not mandatory, nor encouraged. After regaining the natural color of the hair, reapplying the solution is done when the reappearance of white hairs is observed, this differs from person to person.

The Forget White formula is intended for application on the scalp, but it can also be applied on other parts of the body. It can be used by pregnant or breastfeeding women. To maximize the beneficial effect, during application we do not recommend shaving the head, but keeping the hair cut at least 0.5 cm from the scalp.

Warnings:

Follow the instructions for use. Product intended for external use only. If it is swallowed, it can rarely cause allergic reactions to the ingredients. If it comes into contact with the eyes, rinse with warm water. The product has not been tested on animals. To keep in a cool place. Do not use the product if you are allergic to any of the ingredients. Test the product on a small portion of the skin, and if no allergic reactions occur in 48 hours, continue using it, otherwise you can return the product no later than 72 hours after receiving the package, with the refund of its value. To be used preferably within one month after opening. Expiry date: see packaging Cosmetic product for hair made in the USA
